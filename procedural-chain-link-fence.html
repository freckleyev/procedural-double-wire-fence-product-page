Turn curves into Chain Link Fence with Geometry Nodes

Custom chain link fence in seconds

Add chain link fence from the Asset Library and adjust its curve, or apply the Geometry Nodes setup to your own curve for instant results.

Fence height

With fully procedural geometry, adjusting the fence height is simple and flexible.

Grid size

Change the grid size effortlessly within seconds.

Wire thickness

The grid adapts to wire thickness changes, offsetting by the exact amount for realistic results.

Poles density

Easily adjust the number of poles along the fence length.

Poles thickness

Effortlessly modify the thickness of vertical and horizontal poles.

Faux Hedge Slats

Procedural Faux Hedge Slats can be added to the chain link fence for a more attractive result, with full control over their design through adjustable parameters.

Privacy inserts

Another insert option is flat privacy inserts. Their size automatically adapts to the grid size and wire thickness, and their material can be customized in the Shader Editor.

Fence materials
